Usage Tips

  • Try search preq:MAT135 to look for courses that have MAT135 as a pre-requisite.

  • Other search prefixes include: excl (exclusion), post (post-requisite), creq (co-requisite).

  • Try search time:monday time:14:00-16:00 to look for courses on Monday 2-4 pm.
